Vegetable Stir-Fry

Description

This colorful Vegetable Stir-Fry is a quick and healthy dish that features a variety of fresh vegetables sautéed in a savory soy-ginger sauce. It's perfect for a light lunch or dinner and pairs well with rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, cooking until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, and carrot. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil.
  5. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, until the sauce is heated through and slightly thickened.
  7. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.
