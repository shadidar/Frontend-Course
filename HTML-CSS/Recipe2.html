Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced into strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Steps:

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken strips and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes until the sauce starts to thicken.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese, dried basil, and dried oregano. Cook until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  6. Add cooked chicken and fettuccine pasta to the skillet. Toss to coat the pasta and chicken with the sauce. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley.
