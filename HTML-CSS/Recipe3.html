Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 115g dark chocolate
  • 115g unsalted butter
  • 150g powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 65g all-purpose flour
  • Butter and cocoa powder for greasing ramekins

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water.
  3. Stir in powdered sugar until smooth.
  4. Whisk in eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  5. Fold in flour until just combined.
  6. Butter and dust four ramekins with cocoa powder.
  7. Divide the batter among the ramekins and place on a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 12-14 minutes, until the edges are set but the centers are still soft.
  9. Let cool for 1 minute, then run a knife around the edges and invert onto plates.
  10. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
