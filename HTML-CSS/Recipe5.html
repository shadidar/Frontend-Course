Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring a rich and creamy sauce made with eggs, cheese, and pancetta. It's quick, comforting, and perfect for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced pancetta and cook until crispy. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat with the garlic and pancetta.
  6. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped parsley.
  8. Serve immediately.
